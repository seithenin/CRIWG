Paper Submission


The Conference proceedings will be published by Springer as a volume in the Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) series. There are two submission categories:

Full papers:16 pages maximum
Work-in-progress papers:8 pages maximum


We use a double-blind reviewing process. Please, do not include the author's name and affiliation or any other type of information that may disclose the authors in the submitted paper itself. As a result of the reviewing process, the Program Committee may suggest changes in the format and/or the contents of the paper, including the category of the paper. In this case, a paper will be conditionally accepted. The Program Committee Chairs will decide on the final acceptance or rejection based on a second analysis of the revised paper.

Authors should follow the instructions and apply Springer conference paper templates (http://www.springer.com/computer/lncs?SGWID=0-164-6-793341-0). The papers should be submitted using the EasyChair (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=criwg2016) system until the submission deadline.

Poster Paper Submission


Posters will be presented during the conference in the poster session. The proceedings for the Poster papers will be published online separated with the main conference proceedings. All submitted papers will be juried by the program committee.

Poster paper:4 pages maximum (A4)

Authors should follow the instructions and apply Poster and Workshop paper templates. The papers should be submitted in PDF using the EasyChair (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=collabtech2016).
